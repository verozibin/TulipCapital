Who we are

Hedge Fund

Tulip Capital was founded in 2012, bringing together top professional investment managers from leading institutional companies. Tulip’s vision is to create value for investors by identifying opportunities while actively managing risks. Our goal is to outperform Tel-Aviv equities indices. We are constantly reaching strong absolute performance with modest market exposure and significant outperformance on a risk-adjusted basis.

Strategy

Our Strategy
